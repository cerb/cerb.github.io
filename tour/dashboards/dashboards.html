Cerb stays focused on your goals.

The most common type of workspace is a dashboard. Dashboards provide real-time visual insight with collections of customizable widgets. You can choose from pre-built widgets or create your own. Each dashboard responsively adapts to the screen size of whatever device is viewing it (e.g. desktop, laptop, smartphone, tablet, dedicated monitor).
